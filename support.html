Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a match?

Tap the "+" button on the home screen, choose your sport, set the details (location, time, number of players), and publish. Other players nearby can then find and join your match.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings in the app and select "Delete Account." This will permanently remove your profile and all associated data. You can also email us at iclibera@gmail.com to request deletion.

Is Sportsplit free?

Sportsplit is free to download and use. Some optional premium features may be available via in-app subscription.

How does payment tracking work?

Sportsplit helps you keep track of who owes what after a match. It does not process actual payments — it's a tracking tool to settle up with your group.

What data does Sportsplit collect?

See our Privacy Policy for full details. In short: we collect only what's needed to run the app (profile info, match data, messages) and never sell your data.
